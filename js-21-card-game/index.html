How to Play Blackjack

  1. Press PLAY
  2. Place your bet
  3. Press HIT or STAY and try to score higher than the dealer, but lower than 21!

Card Values

  • 2-10: face value
  • Jack, Queen, King: 10
  • Ace: 1 or 11 (dynamically decided)

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Play: Enter
  • Hit: Enter
  • Stay: s
